How to bake a cake

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.
  3. In a bowl, cream together the sugar and butter.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  5. Combine flour and baking powder, then add to the creamed mixture.
  6. Stir in the milk until the batter is smooth.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
  8. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
  10. Remove from the pan and allow it to cool completely before serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
Sugar-
1 cup of white granulated sugar
Butter-
1/2 cup, softened
Eggs-
2 large eggs
Vanilla Extract-
2 teaspoons for flavor-
Flour-
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
Baking Powder-
1 3/4 teaspoons to help the cake rise
Milk-
1/2 cup to smooth the batter
